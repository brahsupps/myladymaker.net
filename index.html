Drip Grade

Each MyLady™ has a style score rating their overall stylisnhness, with each cosmetic scored on its rarity, then weighted against the prominence of its slot, with a multiplier applied for theme or costume complete badges. Style score are designed to help tie value to aesthetics over pure trait scarcity.

Each MyLady™ is given a letter grading for their style score (C, B, A, S, SS) to help categorize the quality of an individual MyLady™.
