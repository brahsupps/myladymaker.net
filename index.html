The bottom border of a MyLady™ card is defined by its theme(s). A MyLady™ whose Hat & Shirt cosmetic share the same theme receives +5 on their Drip Score.

Drip Grade

Each MyLady™ has a drip score rating their overall stylisnhness, with each cosmetic scored on its rarity, then weighted against the prominence of its slot, with a multiplier applied for theme or costume complete badges. Drip score are designed to help tie value to aesthetics over pure trait scarcity.

Each MyLady™ is given a letter grading for their drip score (D, C, B, A, S, SS) to help categorize the quality of an individual MyLady™.
